# Inference Skills Index

This is a collection of skills provided by [inference.sh](https://inference.sh). They give the agent access to hundreds of AI models and specialized workflows.

### Main Capabilities:
- **Image Generation:** Flux, GPT-4, Qwen, Nano-Banana, Background Removal, Upscaling.
- **Video & Animation:** Talking heads, Google Veo, Seedance, HappyHorse, Remotion rendering.
- **Audio & Voice:** ElevenLabs (TTS, STT, Dubbing, Music, Voice Changer), Music generation.
- **LLM & Search:** Specialized RAG pipelines, advanced web search, and model access.
- **Guides:** Design (Logo, Landing Pages), Writing, Social Media, Product Strategy, and Video specs.

To use any of these skills, refer to their specific `SKILL.md` files located in the subdirectories of `.agents/skills/inference-skills/`.
